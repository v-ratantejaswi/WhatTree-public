WhatTree - v1.0

Welcome to WhatTree, an interactive web platform that enables users to explore, contribute, and learn about various tree species around the globe. Utilizing user-submitted photos and data, WhatTree maps tree locations and provides educational insights into their ecological significance and beauty.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote environmental awareness and engagement through citizen science. We aim to build a comprehensive database of tree species, supported by the contributions of nature enthusiasts and experts alike.

Privacy Statement

At WhatTree, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We want to be transparent about the data we collect and how it is used:

  • Data Collection: The images uploaded are stored to display on the web application and the image contains non personally identifiable metadata, so none of the user's personal information is stored.
  • Privacy by Design: Our platform is designed with privacy in mind, ensuring that contributions remain anonymous and personal information is not required or tracked.
